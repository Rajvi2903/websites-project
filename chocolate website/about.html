About Us

ChocoHouse is proud of their history of producing hand-crafted chocolates in flavors and tastes that support both tradition as well as cutting-edge culinary flavors.

The shop has been open for over 30 years and is now a family tradition for several generations of Mercer Islanders. This tradition continues today, as the family delights in making all guests' visits to the shop a delicious experience!
